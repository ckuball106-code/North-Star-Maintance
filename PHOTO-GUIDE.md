# 📸 How to Add Photos to Your Website Gallery

## From Your Phone (While in the Truck!)

### Step 1: Take or Find Your Photo
- Take a photo with your phone camera
- Or select an existing photo from your gallery

### Step 2: Upload to a Free Image Host
Choose one of these easy options:

**Option A: ImgBB (Easiest - No Account Needed)**
1. Go to https://imgbb.com on your phone
2. Tap "Start Uploading"
3. Select your photo
4. Once uploaded, tap "Direct Link" and copy it
5. Done! Use this link in Step 3

**Option B: Google Photos**
1. Upload photo to Google Photos
2. Open the photo and tap Share
3. Tap "Create Link"
4. Copy the link
5. Use this link in Step 3

**Option C: Dropbox**
1. Upload photo to Dropbox
2. Tap Share → Create Link
3. Copy the link
4. Use this link in Step 3

### Step 3: Add to Your Website
1. Open your website: `north-star-site/admin.html`
2. Paste the image URL
3. Add a title (e.g., "Lawn Care - Smith Property")
4. Add a description (optional)
5. Choose the category
6. Tap "Add Photo to Gallery"
7. Done! The photo appears on your website instantly!

## From Your Computer

1. Save photo to your computer
2. Upload to ImgBB.com or your preferred host
3. Open `admin.html` in your browser
4. Follow Step 3 above

## Managing Photos

- **View all photos:** Open `admin.html`
- **Delete a photo:** Click the "Delete" button next to any photo
- **Reorder photos:** Newer photos appear first automatically

## Tips

- Photos appear on the home page gallery (shows 6 most recent)
- Use clear, well-lit photos that showcase your work
- Add descriptive titles so customers know what they're looking at
- Photos are stored in your browser, so they persist between visits

## Troubleshooting

**Photo won't load?**
- Make sure you copied the direct image link (should end in .jpg, .png, etc.)
- Try uploading to ImgBB.com instead - it's the most reliable

**Can't see the admin page?**
- Open: `c:\Users\Christian\Desktop\north-star-site\admin.html`
- Or add `/admin.html` to your website URL

**Photos disappeared?**
- They're stored in your browser's localStorage
- Don't clear your browser data
- Consider backing up by copying photos to a note occasionally
