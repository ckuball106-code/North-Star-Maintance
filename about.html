About North Star Maintenance

Founded on the principles of quality workmanship and reliable service, North Star Maintenance has been serving the Slayton area and surrounding communities with pride. We understand that your home or lake property is more than just a building—it's where memories are made.

Our experienced team brings years of expertise in property maintenance, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. Whether it's maintaining your lawn through the growing season or ensuring safe access during winter storms, we're committed to being your trusted partner in property care.

We specialize in both residential homes and lake properties, understanding the unique needs each requires. Our goal is simple: keep your property looking its best so you can focus on what matters most.
