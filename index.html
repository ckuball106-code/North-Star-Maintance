Service Details

Learn more about our comprehensive property maintenance services.

Lawn Care

Our lawn care services are designed to keep your property looking pristine throughout the growing season. We offer:

  • Weed Eating: Precise trimming around edges, trees, docks, and hard-to-reach areas to maintain clean lines.
  • Spraying: Targeted weed control and fertilization to promote healthy turf growth.
  • Seeding: Overseeding and patch repair to thicken and improve your lawn's appearance.
  • Mowing: Professional mowing with optional edging for a polished finish.
  • Trimming & Edging: Crisp lines along sidewalks, driveways, and landscaping beds.
  • Spring/Fall Cleanup: Removal of leaves, sticks, and seasonal debris.
Pressure Washing

Restore the beauty of your property's exterior surfaces with our professional pressure washing services:

  • Driveway Washing: Remove dirt, oil stains, and grime from concrete and asphalt surfaces.
  • House Washing: Gentle cleaning of siding to remove dirt and mildew buildup.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clear leaves and debris to prevent water damage and maintain proper drainage.
  • Deck/Patio Washing: Refresh outdoor living spaces for a clean, inviting appearance.
  • Sidewalks & Steps: Improve safety and appearance of walkways.
  • Dock/Boathouse Washing: Specialized cleaning for lakefront properties.
Junk Removal

We provide efficient junk removal services for residential and commercial properties:

  • Garage Cleanouts: Clear out accumulated items and create organized storage space.
  • Basement Cleanouts: Remove clutter and prepare spaces for better use.
  • Rental Property Cleanouts: Thorough cleaning between tenants.
  • Lake Property Cleanouts: Specialized service for seasonal and year-round lake homes.

Available in multiple load sizes: Small Load ($125), ¼ Truck ($195), ½ Truck ($325), ¾ Truck ($475), Full Truck ($625).

Handyman Services

Our skilled handymen handle a wide range of repair and installation projects:

  • Small Repairs: Fix leaky faucets, doors, windows, and other household issues.
  • Installations: Shelves, fixtures, hardware, and home improvements.
  • Punch Lists: Complete final touches on new construction or renovations.

Please provide details of your project when requesting a quote.

Window Washing

Crystal clear windows enhance your home's appearance and let in more natural light:

  • Interior & Exterior Cleaning: Thorough cleaning of all window surfaces.
  • Screen Cleaning: Remove dust and debris from window screens.
  • Track Cleaning: Clear tracks and frames for smooth operation.

Pricing based on window size and story: Small ($8-12), Medium ($12-16), Large/Picture ($18-26).

Snow Removal

Keep your property safe and accessible during winter with our snow removal services:

  • Driveway Clearing: Prompt removal of snow to ensure safe access.
  • Sidewalk Clearing: Clear pathways for pedestrian safety.
  • Salting: Apply ice melt to prevent slippery conditions.

Starting prices: Driveway ($50), Sidewalks ($30), Salting ($25).

Concrete Work

Professional concrete services for residential and commercial properties:

  • Driveway Installation: New concrete driveways with proper grading and reinforcement.
  • Patio Installation: Custom concrete patios for outdoor living spaces.
  • Sidewalk Installation: Durable sidewalks and walkways.
  • Concrete Repair: Fix cracks, chips, and damaged sections.
  • Concrete Overlay: Apply decorative overlay to existing concrete surfaces.
  • Steps/Stairs: Safe and sturdy concrete steps and stairways.

Pricing varies by project size and scope. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific needs.

Seasonal Property Care Tips

🌱 Spring & Summer

  • Start early: Schedule spring cleanup to remove winter debris
  • Regular mowing: Keep grass at 2.5-3 inches for healthiest growth
  • Pressure wash: Clean siding and decks before the season heats up
  • Inspect: Check for winter damage and address repairs early
  • Lake properties: Open seasonal properties and inspect docks

🍂 Fall & Winter

  • Fall cleanup: Remove leaves to prevent lawn damage
  • Gutter cleaning: Clear debris before winter to prevent ice dams
  • Winterize: Prepare lake properties for cold weather
  • Snow prep: Set up snow removal service before the first storm
  • Safety first: Keep walkways clear and salted for safety
