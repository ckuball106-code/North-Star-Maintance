Lawn Care

Our lawn care services are designed to keep your property looking pristine throughout the growing season. We offer:

  • Weed Eating: Precise trimming around edges, trees, docks, and hard-to-reach areas to maintain clean lines. Starting at $35
  • Spraying: Targeted weed control and fertilization to promote healthy turf growth. Starting at $45
  • Seeding: Overseeding and patch repair to thicken and improve your lawn's appearance. Starting at $85
  • Mowing: Professional mowing with optional edging for a polished finish.
  • Trimming & Edging: Crisp lines along sidewalks, driveways, and landscaping beds. Starting at $30
  • Spring/Fall Cleanup: Removal of leaves, sticks, and seasonal debris. Starting at $120
Pressure Washing

Restore the beauty of your property's exterior surfaces with our professional pressure washing services:

  • Driveway Washing: Remove dirt, oil stains, and grime from concrete and asphalt surfaces.
  • House Washing: Gentle cleaning of siding to remove dirt and mildew buildup.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clear leaves and debris to prevent water damage and maintain proper drainage.
  • Deck/Patio Washing: Refresh outdoor living spaces for a clean, inviting appearance.
  • Sidewalks & Steps: Improve safety and appearance of walkways.
  • Dock/Boathouse Washing: Specialized cleaning for lakefront properties.
Junk Removal

We provide efficient junk removal services for residential and commercial properties:

  • Garage Cleanouts: Clear out accumulated items and create organized storage space.

  • Lake Property Cleanouts: Specialized service for seasonal and year-round lake homes.

Available in multiple load sizes: Small Load ($125), ¼ Truck ($195), ½ Truck ($325), ¾ Truck ($475), Full Truck ($625).

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote?

Getting a quote is easy! Simply select the services you need from our Services section, add them to your cart, and submit a quote request. You can also call us directly at 507-920-6409 to discuss your needs.

Do you offer recurring service plans?

Yes! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly service plans for lawn care and other maintenance services. Recurring plans come with discounted rates—up to 15% off for weekly service.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Slayton, MN and serve the surrounding communities including Lake Shetek, Currie, Fulda, Westbrook, Tracy, and more. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.

Do you provide services for lake properties?

Absolutely! We specialize in lake property maintenance including dock washing, shoreline cleanup, seasonal opening/closing services, and regular upkeep for vacation homes.

Are you insured?

Yes, North Star Maintenance is fully insured for your peace of mind. We take safety seriously and maintain comprehensive liability coverage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and major credit cards. Payment is typically due upon completion of service, though we can arrange other terms for recurring customers.

We understand emergencies happen! While we can't always guarantee same-day service, we do our best to accommodate urgent requests. Call us at 507-920-6409 and we'll work with you.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with our work, please let us know within 48 hours and we'll make it right—guaranteed.

Seasonal Property Care Tips

🌱 Spring & Summer

  • Start early: Schedule spring cleanup to remove winter debris
  • Regular mowing: Keep grass at 2.5-3 inches for healthiest growth
  • Pressure wash: Clean siding and decks before the season heats up
  • Inspect: Check for winter damage and address repairs early
  • Lake properties: Open seasonal properties and inspect docks

🍂 Fall & Winter

  • Fall cleanup: Remove leaves to prevent lawn damage
  • Gutter cleaning: Clear debris before winter to prevent ice dams
  • Winterize: Prepare lake properties for cold weather
  • Snow prep: Set up snow removal service before the first storm
  • Safety first: Keep walkways clear and salted for safety
